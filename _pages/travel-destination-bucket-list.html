Prior to embarking on our round the world trip back in 2008 (which you can read about here), I had little interest in international travel - to be honest I really wanted to explore what we have here in Australia.

Very early on into our trip however, I had been well and truly bitten by the travel bug. Now a few years on I've started to put together my travel bucket list - the list of places I would really like to see in my time.

As you can read about in the blog I have already put ticks in some of the boxes, but it's a big world and there are still lots of place I want to visit... and just as many that I want to go back to.

The Bucket List:

  • Germany - done
  • England - done

  • Spain - done
  • Italy - done
  • Turkey - done
  • Vietnam
